Idly-Chutney

Description:

Idly-chutney is a very famous tiffin in South India.

I really love them so much

Ingredients:

  • idly
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/4 cup of chutney

Steps:

  1. Add 2 cups of idly to a blender.
  2. Pour in 1 cup of milk.
  3. Add 1/4 cup of chocolate syrup.
  4. Blend until smooth.
  5. Pour into a glass and serve.
