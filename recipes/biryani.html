Mutton Biryani

Description:

Mutton Biryani is a very famous meal

I really love them so much

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/4 cup of chocolate syrup

Steps:

  1. Add 2 cups of vanilla ice cream to a blender.
  2. Pour in 1 cup of milk.
  3. Add 1/4 cup of chocolate syrup.
  4. Blend until smooth.
  5. Pour into a glass and serve.
